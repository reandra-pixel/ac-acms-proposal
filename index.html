ACMS | Website Design & Development Proposal

A tailored website solution for Automated Care Management Solutions. Questions or concerns? Do not hesitate to contact our team.

ACMS is a healthcare technology company supporting organizations that manage high-risk, high-cost, and clinically complex patient populations. Your platform and services support programs including remote patient monitoring, chronic care management, transitional care management, and broader population health and care coordination initiatives.

Your target buyers — health plans, health systems, ACOs, MSOs, and physician organizations — expect a digital presence that reflects the trust, credibility, and sophistication of the healthcare and healthcare SaaS markets. With ACMS approaching commercial readiness, now is the ideal time to build a modern, conversion-oriented website that clearly communicates your value proposition and supports lead generation through strong user experience and content hierarchy.

Our recommended approach focuses on transforming your existing wireframe and draft content into a polished, professional digital presence — optimized for responsiveness, accessibility, performance, SEO, and ease of content updates — with a scalable architecture that supports future growth and solution expansion.

Technology Stack

Three Approaches for ACMS

Based on your requirements for flexibility and in-house content management, we offer three proven approaches — each delivering full content editing capabilities for your team with different strengths depending on your priorities.

Option A

Static Site + Netlify CMS

A streamlined, high-performance static site deployed on Netlify with a built-in CMS editor. Your team gets an intuitive content management interface to update pages, publish blog posts, and manage media — while the site itself loads instantly with zero server overhead.

  • Lightning-fast load times — static files served from a global CDN
  • Built-in CMS editor — no code knowledge required for content updates
  • Enterprise-grade security — no database to hack, no plugins to exploit
  • Zero hosting costs — Netlify's free tier handles most business traffic
  • Ideal for: MVP launch, budget-conscious builds, fast turnaround
Option B

WordPress CMS

The industry-standard content management system powering over 40% of the web. A familiar, proven platform with a massive plugin ecosystem — giving your team a user-friendly dashboard to manage every aspect of the site without touching code.

  • Most widely adopted CMS — easy to find support, tutorials, and developers
  • Intuitive admin dashboard — your team can update pages, publish posts, and manage media with minimal training
  • Massive plugin ecosystem — thousands of extensions for forms, SEO, email marketing, and more
  • Flexible hosting options — from managed WordPress hosts to your own infrastructure
  • Ideal for: Teams who want a familiar CMS, frequent content updates, plugin-based extensibility
Option C

Custom React/Next.js + Headless CMS

A fully custom-built application using React and Next.js, paired with a headless CMS (such as Sanity, Contentful, or Strapi) for content management. This gives you maximum flexibility for interactive features, custom animations, and deeper platform integrations down the road.

  • Native animations & interactivity — platform demos, animated explainers, dynamic content
  • Headless CMS — your team edits content through a clean dashboard; developers control the frontend
  • API-first architecture — seamless integration with your existing HIPAA-compliant systems
  • Future-proof — easily expand into client portals, interactive demos, or mobile apps
  • Ideal for: Premium builds, complex integrations, long-term scalability
